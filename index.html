Description

Elden Ring is a 2022 action RPG developed by FromSoftware, directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, and featuring worldbuilding by George R. R. Martin. Set in the open world of the Lands Between, players create a character to restore the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord. The game offers a third-person perspective, exploration of six main areas on horseback, and a mix of combat, stealth, and magic. Key features include checkpoints for fast travel, rune-based upgrades, and both cooperative and PvP multiplayer modes. The game, which drew inspiration from Dark Souls, was acclaimed for its expansive world and gameplay, winning multiple Game of the Year awards and selling 25 million copies. Its DLC, Shadow of the Erdtree, released in June 2024, continued this success with over five million copies sold.
