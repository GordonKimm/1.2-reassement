Main Bosses

Margit

Margit, the Fell Omen is a Great Enemy Boss in Elden Ring. This is an optional boss as players don't need to defeat him to advance in Elden Ring, though they do need to defeat Margit to advance to Stormveil Castle.

Godrick

Godrick the Grafted, also known as Godrick the Golden, is a Demigod Boss in Elden Ring.

Rennala

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon is a Legend Boss in Elden Ring.

Radahn

Starscourge Radahn is a Legend Boss in Elden Ring. Starscourge Radahn is heralded as one of the mightiest living demigods.

Rykard

God-Devouring Serpent and Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy is a two-phase Demigod Boss in Elden Ring. The God-Devouring Serpent is a massive coiled serpent found at the Volcano Manor.

Draconic Tree Sentinel

Draconic Tree Sentinel is an overworld Boss in Elden Ring. Draconic Tree Sentinel is a massive knight clad in golden armor that rides a massive horse and wields draconic magic.

Morgot

Morgott, The Omen King, also known as Morgott the Grace Given, is a Demigod Boss in Elden Ring.

Godskin Duo

Godskin Duo is a Boss in Elden Ring. In this dual boss fight, you'll have to fight against a Godskin Apostle and a Godskin Noble simultaneously, as each one resurrects the other.

Maliketh

Beast Clergyman and Maliketh, The Black Blade is a two-phase Legend Boss in Elden Ring. Gurranq, Beast Clergyman is found at the end of Crumbling Farum Azula and is revealed to be Maliketh, the Black Blade.

Radagon

Radagon of the Golden Order is a Boss in Elden Ring. Radagon is a tall, fractured god wielding the hammer that shattered the Elden Ring, and is found in the heart of the Erdtree.

Elden Beast

Elden Beast is a Boss in Elden Ring. Elden Beast is the true form of the Elden Ring, acting as the vassal of the Greater Will, and is found in the Elden Throne.

Mohg

Mohg, Lord of Blood is a Demigod Boss in Elden Ring. Unlike his twin Morgott, Mohg fully embraced his accursed Omen blood.

Malenia

Malenia, Blade of Miquella and Malenia, Goddess of Rot is a two-phase Demigod Boss in Elden Ring. She is the twin sister of Miquella and gained renown for her legendary battle against Starscourge Radahn.

Astel

Astel, Naturalborn of the Void is encountered in a cavern beyond the Lake of Rot, accessed via a coffin in the Grand Cloister.

Placidusax

Dragonlord Placidusax is a Legend Boss in Elden Ring. This massive, two-headed dragon is found in Crumbling Farum Azula. He reigned as the current Elden Lord at some time before the reign of Marika.
